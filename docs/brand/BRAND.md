# iGentic Brand Foundation

Last updated: 2026-06-12

## Brand decision

The master brand is **iGentic**.

`iGentic iPhone` is the first focused runtime track, but the long-term identity is device-agnostic: a privacy-first control layer for personal AI systems.

## Core idea

**Human control at the center. Intelligence around it. Policy before action.**

The brand must communicate that iGentic is not another chatbot and not a cloud-first automation product. It is an open-source research project for local-first personal AI control.

## Brand promise

> Private AI that acts only within the boundaries you control.

## Positioning

- **Category:** personal AI runtime / trust-control layer
- **Primary platform today:** iPhone research prototype
- **Future platform direction:** personal-device runtime layer across iPhone, Mac and trusted local workers
- **Audience:** privacy-conscious developers, Swift/iOS builders, local-AI researchers, security reviewers and early community contributors
- **Tone:** calm, precise, transparent, safety-first

## Design principles

1. **Do not imitate Apple.** Be compatible with the expectations of high-quality Apple-platform software without using Apple trade dress, logos, product silhouettes or campaign styling.
2. **Make control visible.** Approval, policy, audit and delegation states should be obvious in UI and diagrams.
3. **Keep the human in the loop.** The identity should show that AI surrounds and supports the user, not the other way around.
4. **Prefer diagrams over hype.** Explain systems, flows and decisions visually.
5. **Open-source clarity.** Contributors should immediately understand where decisions happen and how to participate.

## Logo concept

The logo mark should combine:

- a small `i` as identity, user and local device,
- an open `G` as guardrail, control ring and agent loop,
- a subtle connector line as policy-gated delegation,
- rounded, minimal geometry,
- quiet silver-blue gradients.

The mark should never be a robot head, generic AI sparkle, chat bubble, Apple logo, iPhone outline, Dynamic Island, camera bump or Siri-like orb.

## Naming rules

Use:

- `iGentic` for the master brand.
- `iGentic iPhone` for the current research track.
- `iGentic Runtime` for the technical system when platform-neutral language is better.
- `iGentic contributors` for community attribution.

Avoid:

- names that start with Apple trademarks,
- names that imply Apple endorsement,
- direct copies of Apple feature names,
- platform-specific wording when the concept is broader than iPhone.

## Visual mood

- Airy white space
- Graphite text
- Frosted silver surfaces
- Subtle blue glow
- Thin technical lines
- Rounded corners
- Calm architecture diagrams
- Clear status badges for policy states

## What the brand should make people feel

- This is careful, not reckless.
- This is technical, but not hostile.
- This is privacy-first, not surveillance-first.
- This is open-source, but still intentional.
- This can become serious IP without closing the community.
